Shutao Cao

Assistant Professor of Economics

Trent University

Welcome. I am an economist specialized in macroeconomics. My research aims to

  • assess distributional effects of macroeconomic policy.

  • understand barriers to productivity growth.

  • understand implications of optimal choices over the life cycle.

Issues I have studied include welfare cost of inflation, financial frictions facing small businesses, and production networks.
