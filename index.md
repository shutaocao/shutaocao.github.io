#### About me

I am an assistant professor in economics at [Trent University](https://www.trentu.ca/). My research is in macroeconomics, with a focus on heterogeneous-agent models. I also apply structural estimation to understanding firm dynamics and productivity growth, as well as life-cycle choices.

At Trent University, I teach courses in macroeconomics, and in quantitative methods including machine learning in economics.
